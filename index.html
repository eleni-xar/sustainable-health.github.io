Intro

I love mathematics, and I enjoy sharing this passion with children. I believe that mathematics can be a fun and creative activity. My goal is to help my students discover mathematical knowledge for themselves, using their own experience as a starting point. Moreover, I teach for understanding, looking for the why and not only the how of mathematical procedures.
